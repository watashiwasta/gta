Game Rant Press & Events 

A leading video game authority covering every corner of gaming culture. From breaking video game news, insightful reviews, entertaining interviews, and the latest in streaming culture, Game Rant is a go-to-destination for savvy game enthusiasts.

With over 10 full years covering the games industry, Game Rant is a trusted primary resource for anything and everything gaming-related - thanks to an editorial team comprised of industry experts and seasoned journalists. Game Rant articles, editors, and quotes have been featured or sourced at the Internet’s biggest and most authoritative news and information outlets.
