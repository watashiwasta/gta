About How to Careers

Work With Us!

Game Rant is an authority-driven site dedicated to delivering high quality news, detailed guides, informative interviews, thoughtful features, expert reviews, and more.

Game Rant pursues every story, whether it’s about a niche indie or popular AAA title, and seeks to ensure fans don’t only hear the news—but understand it. We were among the first to report on Overwatch 2 requiring a phone number to play, we broke the news on Assassin’s Creed Mirage not featuring playable modern-day elements, and we’ve spoken with Bella Ramsey and Pedro Pascal about portraying Joel and Ellie in the Last of US HBO series.

At Game Rant, our readership comes first. The editorial team seeks to foster every idea that comes through our doors, ensuring the most accurate news and informative features are made available to our readers.

How To Write For Game Rant

Game Rant is always looking to hire passionate individuals with expertise in the industry, but our positions also support those looking to get their start in the industry. The specifics of each position are detailed below.

Fill out one of the applications, and once hired, we’ll issue a training module, invite you into the Game Rant portal, and set you up with the lead editor of your department.

What we need from you

  • Do you have a passion for writing and video games, and want to use your experience with both?
  • Do you have good communication skills and work well with others to produce the best written content possible?
  • Are you capable of providing original insight and informed editorializing for readers?
